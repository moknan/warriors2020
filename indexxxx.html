About Us

TINY HEREOS: is a collection of 1000 unique and captivating tiny pixel heroes each with their own distinct personality and backstory. Collect them all and own a piece of heroic history!

history

Long ago, in a distant pixelated world, there were 1000 tiny heroes who lived among the people. These heroes were born with a special power that allowed them to harness the energy of the pixels that made up their world. They were known as Pixel Knights, and they dedicated their lives to protecting the realm from evil forces.

Each Pixel Knight had a unique appearance and set of skills that made them stand out from the others. Some were skilled in sword fighting, while others were masters of archery or magic. But all of them shared a deep sense of justice and a commitment to defending the innocent.

As the years went by, the Pixel Knights faced many challenges. They battled fierce monsters, rescued princesses, and even traveled through time to stop villains from changing history. But through it all, they remained steadfast in their mission to protect the people of their world. Now, the Pixel Knights have been immortalized as NFTs, each one representing a tiny piece of the heroic legacy that they left behind. Collectors who own these NFTs will have a piece of the Pixel Knights' story to call their own, and a reminder of the bravery and heroism that they embodied.
